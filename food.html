My Food Preferences

Food isn’t just a part of my life—it’s the part. It’s how I celebrate, how I dream, and sometimes, how I get myself into a little trouble (who knew stealing a whole pile of sel-roti was frowned upon?). But let’s be real—not all foods are equal, and not all flavors earn their spot in my bowl. Some make my tail wag faster than you can say “good boy,” while others… well, they’re better left to the humans. Every bite tells a story, and these are the ones that stand out—whether I love them or hate them.
